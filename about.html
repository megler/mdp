About Maison du Pup

I am so glad you decided to join me on my journey to find the best dog-themed people stuff!

My job is to bring you dog related treasures from my Adventures In Shopping, stories that will make you laugh (I'm a strict NO "heartwarming" tearjerker zone!), and generally a dog inspired good time.

You may be wondering what's the idea behind Maison du Pup?

Let me tell you a story...

It actually all started with an addiction to cat tee shirts.

I know.  I know.  CATS.

But there were some REALLY COOL tees and I couldn't get enough of them!  I wore those bad boys everywhere.  And I do mean everywhere.

Well as you can imagine, the dogs were not impressed with my wardrobe choices.

What is a dog and a cat mom to do?

An internet inspired road trip, of course!

Like Dorothy (you'll never see me in gingham) and Toto (Gusto just glared at me) on the yellow brick road, I set off to find awesome dog t-shirts.

And I found them.  Oh I found them PLUS some!

And that led to a problem...

Whatever would I do with all I found?  Whomever would I show them to?  (wait, that's Scarlett, not Dorothy.  You will never see me in a dress made of drapes, either).

Well after some soul searching and bank account checking, I decided I would bring all these great finds to you.

And that, my friends, is all about Maison du Pup! I hope you like what you find! Check back often, we bring on new designs every week!

Until we meet agian,

Marceia "Gingham Clashes With My Hat" Egler
